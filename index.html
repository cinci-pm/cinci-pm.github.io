Cincinnati Perl Mongers

We are a local Perl Mongers group for the greater Cincinnati area. We meet monthly for social gatherings and presentations.

The Mailing List

If you are interested in joining Cincinnati.pm, please join the low volume mailing list.

IRC

You can join us in the #cinci.pm channel on irc.perl.org.

Meetings

We meet monthly and the times and locations are announced on the mailing list.
